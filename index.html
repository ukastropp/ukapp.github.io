UK Astro-Particle Phenomenology (UK APP)

The UK APP conference series gathers experts in astroparticle, early universe, and beyond Standard Model phenomenology. Meetings are organised semi-annually and hosted by different institutes across the UK.

The next UK APP conference will be hosted by King's College London, and take place in the Spring 2025.
